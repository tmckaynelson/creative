So you have found me, McKay Nelson. I am an aspiring web developer. Before I took my first programming class if you would have asked me if I wanted to be a developer I would have said no. In fact, I had been asked that question and the prospect of sitting behind a screen reading lines of gibberish did not seem appealing to me. After taking my first programming class however I found I thoroughly enjoy writing code. After spending a couple of years working as a Quality Assurance Intern on a company's web development I decided I wanted to do web development.
